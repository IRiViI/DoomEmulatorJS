Artificial recipes





Computers don't need food. That's great and all but we do! So make me a sandwich! Or some other food item please.

A text generation model GPT-2 is finetuned to predict the next word of a recipe. During the training it generates a title, categories, the ingredients, the instructions and a review in that order.

So pick one you like and make it yourself! But use our commens sense to verify that the food is safe to eat.

Doom




You rather want to create your one frames instead of playing the game? I got you covered fam.



There are many examples of deep learning models playing computer games. But not so many deep learning models emulating a computer game.

This application is running a neural network that predicts the next frame based on the previous few frames and the key board inputs.

It makes use of an autoencoder model in order to encode and decode the frames to and from a condense latent space. And a recurrent neural network is used to extrapolate these lantent spaces to determine the next state.
